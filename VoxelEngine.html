Voxel Engine

A Voxel Renderer made in C/C++ with OpenGL

This is a voxel rendering engine that I made with OpenGl/GLFW/CGLM all done in C/CPP. I used C for the low level structures and funcitons and made a little library for myself, then I used C++ for the higher level concepts such as a camera system.
